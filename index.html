About TGHI

Our Vision

A future where every first-generation learner and marginalized youth in Tanzania has equal access to guidance, education, skills, and the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower first-generation learners and underserved youth through mentorship, skills development, inclusive digital education, and climate-conscious community engagement.

Our Core Values

Equity

We believe in fair opportunities for all — regardless of background.

Empowerment

We equip youth with the tools, mindset, and support to take control of their future.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership.

Innovation

We adapt, create, and integrate modern solutions to tackle youth challenges.

Our Journey

2024

Concept Development

Initial research and concept development for TGHI, identifying the unique challenges faced by first-generation learners in Tanzania.

Early 2025

Foundation Established

Formal establishment of The Guiding Hand Initiative with a clear vision and mission to serve marginalized youth.

Mid 2025

Program Development

Development of core programs focusing on mentorship, digital skills, climate action, and community engagement.

Present

Implementation & Growth

Launching programs and building partnerships to expand our reach and impact across Tanzania.

Founder's Note

What It Truly Means to Be of Service - The Heart of TGHI

"We serve not for recognition, but because we know how it feels to lack support—and we choose to become the kind of help we once wished for."

— Barnaba Zakaria Madirisha, Founder & Executive Director

Service from the Heart, Not the Ego

Anyone can serve. But not all service is genuine. Sometimes, people help others to be seen, praised, or remembered - not necessarily to make a real difference.

At TGHI, we constantly ask ourselves: Are we serving to be noticed? Or are we truly trying to understand, uplift, and walk with others through their challenges?

Our Approach to Genuine Service

We aim to serve first-generation learners and marginalized youth with honesty, dignity, and humanity.

  • Reflecting on our intentions
  • Practicing empathy
  • Staying humble
  • Focusing on real needs

Our Programs & Focus Areas

Mentorship & Role Modelling

Peer and expert mentorship programs, school-based support systems to guide first-generation learners through their educational journey.

Digital & Practical Skills

Computer literacy, entrepreneurship, job readiness, robotics, drone technology, and innovation workshops to prepare youth for the future.

Climate Action & Sustainability

Youth-led environmental projects, education on climate change, clean energy, and green entrepreneurship.

Health & Well-being

Mental health awareness, SRHR and nutrition sessions, particularly for adolescent girls and young mothers.

Community Engagement

School-community dialogues, engaging with local leaders and partners to improve youth policies and advocacy.

Partnership Building

Building a network of grassroots and global partners to amplify our impact and reach more marginalized youth.
